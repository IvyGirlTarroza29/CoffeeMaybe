About Us

Coffee Maybe Store is a cozy café nestled in the heart of the city. We pride ourselves on serving the finest coffee blends sourced from around the world, accompanied by delicious pastries and snacks. It’s quite easy to locate, though.Once you’re there, you get to enjoy a nice outdoor area where they provide tables and chairs for groups of coffee lovers to enjoy.


The name of our shop came from the idea of two people having a conversation about having a coffee which goes like

Guy: Coffee?
Girl: Maybe.

Apparently, it's the introduction to a beautiful love story.
